Flashcards

 For each card add a front that will be your clue and a back that will be what you must remember.

Upon being presented with a new card you will not see the back. After clicking 'Show Answer', you will see the answer and the difficulty buttons will become visible.

The more difficult you find it to remember a card, the more often that card will be shown to you. Likewise the easier a card is, the less it will be repeated.

An example:

Front card: The capital of England

Back card: London
